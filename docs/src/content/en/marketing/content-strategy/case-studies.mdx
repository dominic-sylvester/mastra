# Case studies

Case studies show how a specific customer built a specific thing with Mastra. They are the highest-leverage marketing content because they prove the product works in production.

## Structure

Use this structure for every case study:

1. **Customer and problem**: Who the customer is and what they needed to build.
1. **Why Mastra**: The specific reasons they chose Mastra over alternatives.
1. **What they built**: A technical walkthrough of the implementation.
1. **Outcomes**: Concrete results, measured where possible.

## Sourcing

Draft case studies from customer interviews, engineering blog posts, or internal support conversations. Always get written approval from the customer before publishing.
