# Content strategy overview

Mastra's content strategy centers on showing real developers using Mastra to build real things. Every piece of content should teach something, demonstrate a capability, or tell a concrete customer story.

## Key topics

- [**Case studies**](/marketing/content-strategy/case-studies): How to structure and publish customer stories.

## Content principles

- **Concrete over abstract**: Use real examples, working code, and named customers.
- **Teach, don't pitch**: Each post should leave the reader with something they can apply.
- **Link to primary sources**: Reference docs, GitHub, and customer sites rather than restating them.
