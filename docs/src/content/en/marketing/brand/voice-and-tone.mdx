# Voice and tone

Mastra's voice is calm, direct, and technically precise. Use active verbs, short sentences, and concrete examples. Avoid marketing hyperbole and AI buzzwords.

## Voice principles

- **Direct**: State facts. Skip throat-clearing and filler phrases.
- **Technical**: Speak to developers. Use correct terminology without over-explaining.
- **Confident, not boastful**: Let the product speak for itself with working examples.
- **Helpful**: Assume the reader is trying to build something. Give them what they need.

## What to avoid

- Hyperbole like "revolutionary," "cutting-edge," or "next-generation."
- Filler phrases like "simply," "just," or "easy."
- Buzzwords without substance (for example, "supercharge your workflow").
- Overly casual tone or attempts at humor in product copy.
